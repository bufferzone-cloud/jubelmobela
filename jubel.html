My Journey

Every Breath is a Second Chance: The Autobiography of Jubel Mobela

My name is Jubel Mobela, and this is my story—a journey shaped by struggle, lifted by hope, and driven by the belief that every breath is a second chance. From humble beginnings in Luanshya, Zambia, to the foundation of growing tech enterprises, my life has never followed a straight path. But in every setback, I've found renewal. In every hardship, a lesson. And in every moment of uncertainty, a second chance to rise again.

Chapter 1: A Childhood in Motion

I was born on July 21, 2002, in Luanshya, Zambia, during a time of personal and familial transition. When my parents separated at a young age, I began a life marked by movement—both physical and emotional. I first lived with my grandmother, who offered me her warmth and strength. Over the years, I moved between various relatives: from Kitwe to Lusaka, then back to Luanshya each place offering new challenges, new beginnings, and second chances to find stability.


Though life often felt unpredictable, I learned to embrace each new environment. Change became my constant companion, and adaptability became a necessity. My early school years saw similar shifts. I began in a private school in Kitwe, only to transfer to a public school in Nkana East. After my aunt's husband passed away, I moved back and forth between families. But through it all, I remained resilient.


Each move was a breath a new chance to learn, grow, and hold on to the hope that things would get better.


Chapter 2: Education as Escape and Empowerment

Education became my anchor. At Nkana Basic School in Kitwe, from Grade 5 to Grade 7, I poured my energy into academics and graduated with high marks. It was the first time I felt that my hard work could truly open doors for me. I continued my journey at Malela Secondary School, where I sat for my Grade 12 exams in 2018—and passed with excellence.


School was more than a place of learning. It was where I began to imagine a future beyond struggle. Every lesson, every exam, every long walk to class was a breath a second chance to rewrite my future.


Chapter 3: Stepping Into the World

In 2019, I took my first steps into the professional world as a teacher at a private school. For two years, I taught students not just what was in their textbooks, but what I was learning myself—that you could come from little and still dream big. I learned how to communicate, lead, and inspire—skills that would shape the rest of my life.


Then in 2021, I interned at Power Tools Logistics as an auditor. It was my first true exposure to the corporate world. I was now wearing formal shoes, sitting in boardrooms, handling data, and solving real business problems. It felt like I had walked through a door I once thought was closed to me.


Every experience was a new breath. A second chance to discover who I could be.


Chapter 4: The Tech Path and a Turning Point

My love for technology had always been simmering beneath the surface. In 2022, I followed that passion to Northern Technical College (NORTEC) in Ndola, enrolling in a Diploma in Computer Systems Engineering. I poured everything into my studies, earning recognition as the best student year after year. For the first time, I didn’t just feel like I belonged I felt like I was thriving.


But life reminded me that growth often walks hand in hand with grief. Just before graduation in September 2023, I lost my father. The pain was overwhelming, and for a moment, it felt like time stood still. But in the silence that followed, I remembered that each breath was still mine to take—and that meant I had another chance to carry on, in his honor.


His passing didn’t end my journey. It sharpened my purpose.


Chapter 5: Building a Future from the Ground Up

In mid-2024, I joined Zamlift as an IT Technician, working hands-on with systems, networks, and digital infrastructure. It felt like everything I had learned was finally being put to use.


But my ambitions didn’t stop there. I launched BUFFER ZONE, a graphic design and tech company I ran as a side hustle. It was a passion project born out of creativity, curiosity, and a desire to empower small businesses with digital tools.


In early 2025, I took an even bigger leap by founding Zatralo Zambia, an online platform that allows Zambians to easily purchase bus tickets. It was a solution to a problem I had seen firsthand—a digital bridge connecting people with the freedom to travel, plan, and explore.


These businesses were more than ventures. They were my breath my second chance to shape Zambia’s future with innovation.


Chapter 6: Looking Forward

I believe in the power of resilience, the magic of ideas, and the necessity of taking risks. My goal is not just to succeed for myself, but to leave behind systems, platforms, and tools that empower others.


From the child who moved from house to house, to the entrepreneur building digital platforms I am a testament to what happens when you don’t give up. When you see each moment not as an ending, but as an invitation to begin again.


Final Words

Every struggle I’ve faced, every loss I’ve endured, and every success I’ve celebrated has reminded me of one thing:

Every breath is a second chance.
A second chance to learn.
A second chance to grow.
A second chance to build the life you believe in.


“Start small, remain focused, and always be ready to grow.”
Jubel Mobela
